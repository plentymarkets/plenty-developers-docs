= Introducing the plugin cookbook

The plugin cookbook is a collection of recipes for plentymarkets plugin scenarios. On this page, you will find answers to questions that might arise when developing plentymarkets plugins.

*Info*: The cookbook is just getting started. New recipes are already on the way. If you can't find an answer to your question, post your requests and feedback in our link:https://forum.plentymarkets.com/c/plugin-entwicklung[forum^].
